About us

The Staff of Sri Sathya Sai Kindergarten, Singapore warmly welcomes you and your child to our school. The successful operation of the kindergarten requires the cooperation of all concerned, in particular the parents, the children and teachers.

Sri Sathya Sai Kindergarten’s philosophy is based on the view that a balanced education gives importance not only to academic success but also the development of good character. The Education in human values aims in developing each child’s highest potential through the practice of universal values of Truth, Right Conduct, Peace, Love and Non-violence.

History

Sri Sathya Sai Kindergarten is a private kindergarten governed by Board of Directors. It is opened to all children regardless of socio-economic status, ethnic origin or religion. The kindergarten started operating since 2 Jan 2002.
