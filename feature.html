Who We Are

VClassrooms is a Mobile Based Education Management System (MEMS) based Application Designed and developed for educational institutes like Schools, Colleges, Tuitions and academies to manage their day to day activities and schooling functions like Student Management, Online Examination, E Books management, Attendance management with smart devices, Video Conferencing, Virtual Classroom etc.

Core Module

  • Online Lectures
  • E-Books
  • Online Examination
  • Attendance management
  • Classes Management
  • Announcements
  • Exams and Results Management
  • Assignments and homework
  • Event Calendar
  • Online Payments
