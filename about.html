About Us

Introduction

The Staff of Sri Sathya Sai Kindergarten, Singapore warmly welcomes you and your child to our school. The successful operation of the kindergarten requires the cooperation of all concerned, in particular the parents, the children and teachers.

Our Philosophy

Sri Sathya Sai Kindergarten’s philosophy is based on the view that a balanced education gives importance not only to academic success but also the development of good character. The Education in human values aims in developing each child’s highest potential through the practice of universal values of Truth, Right Conduct, Peace, Love and Non-iolence.

As we know that a child learns best within a social environment, which supports each individual’s development. The goal of early childhood education is also to cultivate the child’s own desire to learn.

Curriculum and Character Development

Sri Sathya Sai Kindergarten is licensed by the Ministry of Education, Singapore. The curriculum follows the guidelines provided by Ministry of Education, Singapore. In addition, there is a strong emphasis on integrating Human Values In curricular and extra-curricular activities. The kindergarten’s objective is to develop good character in children as well as achieve academic excellence.

History

Sri Sathya Sai Kindergarten is a private kindergarten governed by Board of Directors. It is opened to all children regardless of socio-economic status, ethnic origin or religion. The kindergarten started operating since 2 Jan 2002.
